## ğŸ“Œ Project Summary

This project is designed to provide comprehensive accounting and tax consultancy services, offering clients a seamless way to manage their financial needs. Users can conveniently access professional support for personal tax consultations, business accounting advice, VAT filing assistance, and tailored financial planning. With integrated features such as appointment booking, invoice management, and other essential tools, the platform ensures a smooth and efficient client experience, making expert financial guidance accessible anytime, anywhere. The mission of this project is to develop a comprehensive, intelligent, and multilingual accounting and tax consultancy platform designed for users. The platform aims to streamline financial management, ensure regulatory compliance, and provide a seamless user experience through automation and advanced AI capabilities.
